- **Aesthetic**: Dark editorial premium (luxury tech founder meets editorial magazine)
- **Typography**: Cormorant Garamond (serif editorial) + Space Grotesk (sans moderna)
- **Colors**: 
  - Background: Deep blue-black (#050508)
  - Accent: Warm amber (#F59E0B)

- **Motion**: HEAVY animations

  - Floating gradients
